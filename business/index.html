Before you join

Clear benefits. Clear limits.

No inflated promises. Cigario is useful when the venue is real, cigar-friendly and worth showing to the community.

What you get

A proper place in Cigario.

  • Your place will become part of the Cigar Community app.
  • Photos, map pin, opening hours, website and smoking details in one clean profile.
  • People who already care about cigars can discover where to go.

What this is not

No fake promise, no automatic listing.

  • Not paid placement. Approval is not guaranteed.

  • Not instant if the listing is incomplete or unclear.
  • Not for places where cigar smoking is not actually welcome.

Listing quality tips

What makes a venue ready for review.

Strong listings are specific, visual and honest. This helps Cigario keep the map useful for guests and fair for venues.

01 Use real venue photos

Upload sharp photos of the lounge, seating, humidor, terrace or smoking area. The first photo becomes the main image.

02 Explain cigar conditions

Tell guests where they can smoke, whether they can bring their own cigars, and what drinks or cigars you offer.

03 Check the map pin

Use the address search, then confirm the marker manually. A correct pin makes the place easier to find in the app.

Cigario Verified

Verified by Cigario is reserved for exceptional places.

Cigario Verified is not automatic. It is awarded only to selected premium venues that we physically visit, evaluate and approve. Think of it as a curated mark for places that genuinely belong on a cigar lover's shortlist.
